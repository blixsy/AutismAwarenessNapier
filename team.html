About Our Team!

Chiara Louise Butt (She/Her)

My name is Chiara Louise Butt. I am the project manager of this project. I am also one of
the sound designers specialising in Dialogue Editing. I decided to work on this project since
it is advertising Autistic Hypersensitivity. This subject is very important to me because I am
an Autistic person in my 20’s and I got my official diagnosis back in 2014.

Fraser Gallagher (He/Him)

Hi! My name is Fraser Gallagher (He/Him) and I’m a 3rd year sound design student.
My main interests in sound design are video games
but I also enjoy doing work for movies and series too.

In my free time I enjoy surfing and rock climbing, and with that I like to integrate a lot of nature based influences into my work!

Brandon Dillon (He/Him)

My name is Brandon Dillon. I am a 3D character and asset artist, currently studying at Edinburgh Napier University. I started
studying Game Design at Fife College, which led my passion for 3D modelling. I studied my HNC
& HNC in 3D modelling.
I am now studying my degree in Animation for Games at Edinburgh Napier University. I am
proficient in the use of 3DS Max, Blender, Substance Painter and Paint.NET.
My hobbies include playing video games, learning as much as I can about 3D modelling, painting
figurine miniatures, listening to music to help me focus and relax with my studies and watching scifi films – something that from an early age
influenced me to work towards finding a job in the animation or games industry.
I was diagnosed with Autism at a young age, which was part of what drew me with this project to
help bring more awareness to autism hypersensitive and the coping mechanisms that are available.
It has been an excellent opportunity to use my skills as a 3D character modeller and animator on
this project by modelling some of the characters, creating shaders for them, and animating some of
the scenes.
I am proud to have worked on this project and to have helped spread more information about neurodiversity.

Guillem Galvia Puigcerver (He/Him)

I'm a final year DMID student based in Edinburgh, with a strong artistic background and a
passion for games, 3D modelling and storytelling.
mainly work in 3D modelling and texturing assets and props, but I also have experience
in 3D character modelling and 3D animation. I am proficient in Blender, Maya, 3DsMax,
Substance Painter, Unreal Engine 5 and Adobe Create.
Currently, I complement my studies with my work as a freelance 3D artist, but I'm looking
for a full-time opportunity where I can showcase my creative skills.

Zakarya Hassan (He/Him)

My name is Zakarya Hassan. I am a third year DMID student based in Edinburgh, with a strong
background and a passion for graphic design, motion graphics and animation.
I mainly work in graphic design and motion graphics, though I have experience in 3D modelling and texturing assets.
I am proficient at softwares such as Photoshop, Premier Pro and After Effects, as well as having learned 3DS Max and Blender.
I joined the group as a Lead Animator for this project. I wanted to.help because the project involved themes based on Autistic Hypersensitivity,
and they required an animator and graphic designer for this.
By joining the group it meant that this would be a great opportunity to showcase my creative skills, but also to provide a message to both neurotypical and neurodivergent
audiences about what a sensory overload can feel like for an autistic person.

Blaine Flynn-Phillips (They/She)

Hello! I'm a third-year student studying web design and development at ENU. I have a deep passion for gaming,
anime, and all things tech-related! My enthusiasm for these interests fuels my creativity, inspiring vibrant
and dynamic designs. Being part of communities that embrace neurodiversity, I saw an incredible opportunity to
broaden my understanding and create a website that celebrates inclusivity and accessibility for all communities.
I'm excited to embark on this journey and create something truly impactful! :)
